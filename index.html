Program Structure

Join our interactive talk program where we discuss common internet problems and brainstorm creative solutions together.

Introduction (5-7 min)

Setting the stage and explaining the program objectives.

Discussion (30-40 min)

Share problems and brainstorm solutions in three key areas.

Wrap-up (5 min)

Summary of ideas and next steps.

Example Topics

Problem

Information Overload & Fake News

"I can never tell if a news article is credible or just clickbait."

Developer Solution

Browser extensions that display credibility scores and fact-checking indicators.

Website Idea

TruthLens - A platform that aggregates news and shows consensus views with bias analysis.

Problem

Digital Clutter & Subscriptions

"I can't track all my subscriptions and what I'm actually paying for."

Developer Solution

Universal subscription management API for centralized control.

Website Idea

SubZen - A dashboard showing all subscriptions with spending analytics and easy cancellation.
